The Solar System

The Sun

The Star of our Solar System

Inclination: 7.005° to Ecliptic | 3.38° to Sun’s equator

Surface gravity: 3.7 m/s² | 0.38 g

Mass: 3.3022×1023 kg | 0.055 Earths

Known satellites: Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Neptune, Uranus, Pluto

Its influence extends far beyond the orbits of distant Neptune and Pluto. Without the sun's intense energy and heat, there would be no life on Earth.
