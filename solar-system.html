The Solar System

The Sun

The Star of our Solar System

Distance from Earth: 1.496×108 km | 8 min 19 s at light speed

Volume: 1.41×1018 km3

Mass: 1.9885 × 1030 kg

Its influence extends far beyond the orbits of distant Neptune and Pluto. Without the sun's intense energy and heat, there would be no life on Earth.
